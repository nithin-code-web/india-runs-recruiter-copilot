# Ranking Strategy

## Positive Signals

- Retrieval Systems
- Ranking Systems
- Recommendation Systems
- Embeddings Experience
- Python
- Vector Databases
- High Assessment Scores
- Active Candidate
- Good Recruiter Response Rate

## Negative Signals

- Marketing titles with AI skills
- Service-company-only background
- Inactive candidates
- Low assessment scores
- Pure research profiles

## Ranking Philosophy

Rank based on demonstrated capability,
not keyword frequency.

Career history is more important than
self-declared skills.

Assessment scores validate claimed skills.

Behavioral signals affect recruiter success.
