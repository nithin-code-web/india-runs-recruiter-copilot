# Results

## Dataset Size

100,000 Candidates

---

## Key Learning

The biggest insight from this challenge:

Career evidence is more reliable than self-reported skills.

A candidate mentioning AI technologies is not necessarily an AI engineer.

Candidates with proven ranking, retrieval, and recommendation system experience consistently ranked higher.

---

## Challenge Faced

Initial ranking relied heavily on keyword matching.

This caused profiles such as:
- Marketing Managers
- Business Analysts
- Project Managers

to rank unexpectedly high.

---

## Solution

Shifted focus toward:

- Career evidence
- AI relevance signals
- Title relevance
- Behavioral signals

instead of simple keyword counting.

---

## Final Top Candidates

Examples:

1. Senior Applied Scientist
2. Senior NLP Engineer
3. AI Engineer
4. Search Engineer
5. Senior AI Engineer

These profiles demonstrated strong evidence of:
- Retrieval systems
- Ranking systems
- Vector databases
- Embedding-based search

---

## Outcome

Successfully ranked 100,000 candidates and surfaced highly relevant AI profiles while filtering unrelated candidates.
